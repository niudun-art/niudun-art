Privacy Policy for Niudun Art Prints

Last updated: 2026-04-23

1. Data Collection

We collect only the minimum necessary data when you connect your Pinterest account:

  • Your Pinterest public profile information (username, display name)
  • Basic content metadata (e.g., pin titles, descriptions) required for content management.
No sensitive personal data (email, location, payment info) is collected.

2. Use of Data

Data is used solely for:

  • Personal content management (organizing and scheduling your Pinterest wall art pins)
  • Improving service features for your own use only.
We do not use Pinterest-derived data for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any user data or Pinterest-derived content with third parties. All data is stored securely and is only accessible to you.

4. Data Retention

Data is retained only for as long as your account is connected. If you disconnect Pinterest access, all your data will be permanently deleted within 7 days.

All data obtained through the Pinterest API will only be used for the purposes stated in this privacy policy, and will not be shared, sold, or disclosed to any third party.
